Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book equipment?

Simply browse available equipment, click on the equipment you need, and click "Book Now". Fill in the booking details and submit your request. The owner will respond within 24 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently only accept cash on delivery. Payment is made directly to the equipment owner when the equipment is delivered to you.

How do I list my equipment?

Register as an equipment owner, go to your dashboard, and click "Add Equipment". Fill in the equipment details, upload photos, and set your rental rates.

What if I need to cancel a booking?

You can cancel bookings up to 24 hours before the scheduled start date. Go to "My Bookings" and click the cancel option. For urgent cancellations, contact support.
